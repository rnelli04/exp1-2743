Season Summary

In a thrilling final that went into the reserve day due to rain, Chennai Super Kings equaled Mumbai's record with their 5th title. Ravindra Jadeja hit a boundary off the last ball to seal the victory against defending champions Gujarat Titans. Shubman Gill had a standout season scoring 890 runs.
