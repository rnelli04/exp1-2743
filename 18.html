Season Summary

The IPL 2025 concluded on June 3 with Royal Challengers Bengaluru defeating Punjab Kings in the final to lift their maiden title. This was the 18th edition of the Indian Premier League - the world's most lucrative franchise-based T20 league. RCB became the eighth team to win the IPL trophy.
