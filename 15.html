Season Summary

This season saw the expansion of the league to 10 teams with the addition of Gujarat Titans and Lucknow Super Giants. Under the captaincy of Hardik Pandya, debutants Gujarat Titans lifted the trophy in their very first season. Jos Buttler dominated with the bat, scoring 4 centuries.
